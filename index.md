# Delete Account & Data – ArivoliAI OET

If you want to delete your ArivoliAI OET account and associated data, please email us from the phone number registered in the app.

**Email:** www.ArivoliAI.com  
**Subject:** Delete my ArivoliAIOET account  
**Include:** Your registered phone number (with country code)

## What will be deleted
- Your account profile data stored in our database (e.g., nickname, coins/points if any)
- Stored app-related records linked to your account (if any)

## What may be retained
- Minimal logs required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance (retained for a limited period)

## Timeline
We process deletion requests within **7–30 days**.
